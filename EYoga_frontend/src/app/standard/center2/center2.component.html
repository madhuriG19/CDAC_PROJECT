DELHI

The Yoga Institute Delhi, an abode of holistic Yoga wellness in the capital of India, 
    shall function under guidance of The Yoga Institute Mumbai, the world’s oldest organised 
    Yoga centre. Founded by Shri Yogendraji, Father of the Modern Yoga Renaissance, 
    The Yoga Institute is recognised worldwide for spreading the awareness of Yoga authentically 
    and preserving the purity of our country’s rich ancient heritage. Headquartered in Mumbai, 
    The Yoga Institute has branches all over India and world.


The Delhi branch shall be run/managed by Shri Yogacharya Sheshpal, 
    who has been teaching and practicing Yoga since 5 decades, 
    along with a team of Government of India (Ministry of AYUSH) certified teachers of 
    The Yoga Institute Mumbai.

    Learn and experience Yoga as a holistic lifestyle to detox your body, mind and soul. 
    You will become more mindful and gain a better understanding of Yoga as a science of 
    awareness and a culture of consciousness. The institute teaches simplified Yoga techniques 
    of asanas and pranayamas that are exclusively designed to suit the demanding lifestyle of a 
    busy householder.

    The principle underlying in our Teacher Training Course is that, first, 
    the perfection of the body helps in the perfection of the mind, and then, 
    when mental efficiency has been gained, the same is used to attain the higher 
    spiritual perfection.

    The Yoga Institute Delhi shall offer 200 hours Teacher’s Training course and other programs 
    along with group Yoga classes. Located in the heart of the nation, which is popular for 
    its enriched culture and heritage, The Yoga Institute Delhi is all set to spread peace, 
    health and joy through Yoga.
